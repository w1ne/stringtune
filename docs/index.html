About this Tuner

Welcome to StringTune, the easiest-to-use online tuner. To start, allow accees to the microphone. Play each string one at a time. The tuner will automatically detect the pitch of the note being played and indicate whether it's sharp or flat. Keep adjusting until the tuner points directly to the center. Click on the fretboard to change instruments. Happy tuning!
